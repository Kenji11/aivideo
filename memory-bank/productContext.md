# Product Context

## Problem Statement
Businesses need high-quality ad videos but face:
- High production costs ($5,000-$50,000 per video)
- Long turnaround times (weeks to months)
- Need for specialized creative teams
- Difficulty iterating on creative concepts

## Solution
An AI-powered video generation pipeline that creates professional 30-second advertisements from simple text prompts in under 10 minutes for under $2 per video.

## How It Works

### User Experience Flow
1. User enters a text prompt (e.g., "Create a sleek ad for luxury watches with gold aesthetics")
2. (Optional) User uploads brand assets (logo, product images)
3. System validates prompt and extracts structured specification
4. User sees real-time progress updates across 6 phases
5. User receives final 1080p 30fps video ready to download
6. Total time: 7-10 minutes

### Key Value Propositions
- **Speed**: 10 minutes vs weeks of traditional production
- **Cost**: $1.76-$3.26 vs thousands of dollars
- **Quality**: Professional 1080p output with consistent styling
- **Flexibility**: 3 ad templates, customizable aesthetics
- **Reliability**: 90%+ success rate with deterministic templates

## Target Use Cases

### 1. Product Showcase
- Focus on product features and details
- Close-up shots, macro details
- Elegant, sophisticated tone
- Use case: Luxury goods, electronics, accessories

### 2. Lifestyle Ad
- Show product in real-world context
- Dynamic pacing, upbeat music
- Bright, vibrant aesthetics
- Use case: Fashion, fitness, consumer goods

### 3. Announcement
- Brand message or campaign launch
- Text-heavy, bold graphics
- Dramatic, impactful tone
- Use case: Events, campaigns, corporate messaging

### For Small Businesses
- Access professional video production
- Compete with larger brands

### For Agencies

- Focus creative energy on strategy

## Constraints & Limitations

### MVP Limitations
- Fixed 30-second duration
- 3 pre-defined templates only
- 1 concurrent video per user
- No iterative editing (generate new video for changes)
- English prompts only

### Technical Constraints
- Generation time: 7-10 minutes (cannot be instant)
- Cost floor: ~$1.76 per video (API costs)
- Quality depends on prompt clarity
- Best for simple, focused messages
