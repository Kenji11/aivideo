# Nike Black Mamba Ad - 30 Second Story

## Prompt for Frontend:

```
Create a powerful and inspiring 30-second lifestyle advertisement video for Nike featuring Kobe Bryant's Black Mamba legacy. The story should open with an intense close-up of a black mamba snake with glowing eyes, transitioning to dynamic basketball action scenes showing the numbers 8 and 24 prominently. Include shots of the iconic Nike Black Mamba basketball shoe with snakeskin texture, Kobe Bryant in action wearing Lakers purple and gold, and the Nike swoosh logo. Use dramatic lighting with dark backgrounds, golden-yellow accents, and purple highlights. The mood should be epic, motivational, and legendary - embodying the Mamba Mentality of relentless pursuit of greatness. Show athletic excellence, determination, and triumph. End with the Nike logo and "Mamba Forever" message.
```

## Story Structure (30 seconds):

1. **0-5s**: Black Mamba snake close-up - intense, powerful opening
2. **5-12s**: Kobe Bryant in action - basketball scenes, numbers 8 and 24 visible
3. **12-20s**: Nike Black Mamba shoe showcase - snakeskin texture, premium details
4. **20-25s**: Dynamic basketball action - Mamba Mentality in motion
5. **25-30s**: Nike logo reveal - "Mamba Forever" finale

## Reference Images to Upload (in order):

1. Black Mamba snake (close-up)
2. Kobe Bryant tribute (with snake, basketball, 8.24)
3. Nike swoosh logo
4. Nike Black Mamba basketball shoe

## Expected Result:

A cinematic 30-second ad that tells the story of Kobe's legacy, the Black Mamba spirit, and Nike's tribute to greatness.
